What is your professional experience?

The first professional experience I had was in Ocala, Florida at a startup named Veterans Trading Company during the summer of 2015. I worked as an Engineering Intern with a focus on software development and databases. My summer-long project digitalized their warehouse receiving operations. I created software that allowed the receiving operators to quickly scan parts with hardware and find them in a database. This allowed for more accurate inspections when searching for counterfeit and fraudulent parts. Afterwards, I had the opportunity to prepare documentation and present my project to an auditor personally. It was a greatly unique experience, and I'm happy to say that I successfully helped the company pass two audits: AS9100 and AS6081.

What's up next for you?

During the Fall, I'm be working as a software developer at GE's Energy Management Division in Melbourne, FL. I was very fortunate to be able to interview with the Aviation team at the SHPE 2015 conference. While they did not have room for me during the fall, they passed me off to the wonderful Energy Management team. I'm working on extremely interesting projects like developing Smart Grids. Furthermore, I'm also a part of the leadership program. By the end of this, I'll have tremendous experience with leading teams and being an all around better professional and engineer.
